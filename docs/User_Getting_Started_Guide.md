# Nex-Gen Lumina — User Getting Started Guide

Congratulations on your new Nex-Gen Lumina permanent LED lighting system! This guide walks you through first-time setup and teaches you how to control every aspect of your lights from the Lumina app.

---

## 1. First-Time Setup

### Step 1: Download the App

Download **Lumina** from:
- **iPhone:** Apple App Store
- **Android:** Google Play Store

### Step 2: Sign In

1. Open the Lumina app
2. Tap **Sign In**
3. Enter the **email** and **temporary password** provided by your installer
4. You will be prompted to **change your password** — choose something memorable and secure

### Step 3: Welcome Wizard

The app walks you through a quick setup:

**A. Network Permission**
- The app needs permission to find your WLED controllers on your WiFi network
- Tap **Allow** when prompted
- On iPhone: A system popup asks for Local Network access — tap **OK**
- On Android: Location permission is needed for WiFi scanning — tap **Allow**

**B. Camera Permission** (optional)
- Used for AR roofline preview and uploading your house photo
- You can skip this and enable it later in Settings

**C. Choose Your Preferred White**
- You'll see 5 white presets displayed on your actual lights:

| Preset | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Warm White** | Cozy, amber-tinted glow (like candlelight) |
| **Soft White** | Gentle, slightly warm tone |
| **Natural White** | Balanced, true-to-life white |
| **Cool White** | Clean, blue-tinted white |
| **Bright White** | Pure white from the dedicated W LED |

- Tap each one to preview it live on your lights
- Select your **primary white** (your go-to everyday look)
- The app automatically suggests a **complementary white** for variety
- You can also create a custom white by adjusting R, G, B, and W sliders

<div class="tip">
<strong>Tip:</strong> Most homeowners prefer Warm White for everyday use and Bright White for task lighting. You can change these anytime in Settings.
</div>

### Step 4: Personalization (First-Run Screen)

If your installer didn't set these during installation, you'll be asked to personalize:

- **Favorite Sports Teams** — Lumina creates game-day lighting automatically
- **Favorite Holidays** — Seasonal themes activate around your chosen holidays
- **Lighting Style** — Slide from "Subtle & Elegant" to "Bold & Energetic"

### Step 5: Your Dashboard

After setup, you land on the **Home Dashboard** — the main control center for your lights.

## 2. The Dashboard — Your Control Center

The dashboard is where you'll spend most of your time. Here's what you'll see:

### Hero Image
The large image at the top shows your house (or a default image until you upload your own). Your lighting controls overlay this image.

### Power Button
The large circular button in the center-right area toggles your lights **on** and **off**. It glows cyan when the lights are on.

### Brightness Slider
The vertical slider lets you adjust brightness from 0% to 100%. Changes take effect instantly on your lights.

### Quick Presets
Small buttons along the right side of the hero image give you one-tap access to your favorites:

| Preset | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Warm White** | Your primary white setting |
| **Bright White** | Your complementary white |
| **Run Schedule** | Activates tonight's scheduled pattern |
| **Custom favorites** | Any patterns you've saved |

<div class="tip">
<strong>Tip:</strong> Long-press a preset to see its details or remove it from your favorites.
</div>

### Smart Suggestions
Below the hero image, Lumina may show contextual suggestions like:
- "Game day tonight — Chiefs colors ready?"
- "Sunset approaching — warm white?"
- "Holiday coming up — Christmas theme available"

Tap a suggestion to apply it instantly.

### Tonight Card
Shows what's scheduled for tonight, with a color preview of the pattern.

## 4. Controlling Your Lights

### Basic Controls (Dashboard)

- **Turn on/off:** Tap the power button
- **Adjust brightness:** Slide the brightness control up or down
- **Apply a preset:** Tap any quick preset button
- **Apply a favorite:** Tap a pattern in the Favorites grid

### Applying a Pattern

1. Go to the **Explore** tab
2. Browse categories: Holidays, Sports, Nature, Mood, Seasonal, and more
3. Tap a category to see available patterns
4. Tap any pattern to **preview it live** on your lights
5. Tap the **heart icon** to save it as a favorite

### Zone Control (Multi-Area Systems)

If your system has multiple lighting areas (e.g., "Front Roofline" and "Side Accent"):

1. Look for the **Channel Selector Bar** at the top of pattern screens
2. Tap a specific area to control only that zone
3. Tap **All** to control everything at once

## 5. Lumina AI — Talk to Your Lights

Lumina is your AI lighting assistant. Access it from:
- The **Lumina tab** (center of bottom bar) for full-screen chat
- The **chat bar** at the bottom of the dashboard for quick commands

### How to Use Lumina

**Type or speak** natural commands. Lumina understands plain English:

| Say This | Lumina Does This |
|----------|-----------------|
| "Turn on warm white" | Applies warm white preset |
| "Chiefs colors" | Sets your lights to red and gold |
| "Make it look like Christmas" | Applies a Christmas theme |
| "Something warm and relaxing" | Chooses a calming warm pattern |
| "Brighten up" | Increases brightness |
| "Turn off" | Powers down the lights |
| "Set a schedule for warm white at sunset" | Creates an automated schedule |
| "Party mode" | Applies an animated, colorful effect |

### Voice Input

1. Tap the **microphone icon** in the chat bar
2. Speak your command
3. Lumina automatically submits after you stop speaking (2-second pause)

<div class="tip">
<strong>Tip:</strong> Lumina learns your preferences over time. The more you use it, the better its suggestions become.
</div>

## 6. Smart Scheduling

### Viewing Your Schedule

1. Tap the **Schedule** tab
2. The week view shows each day with its scheduled patterns
3. Tap a day to see details (pattern name, times, colors)
4. Use arrows to navigate between weeks

### Creating a Schedule

1. On the Schedule tab, tap the **+** button
2. Choose:
   - **Pattern/Scene** — what lights to display
   - **Start Time** — when to turn on (or use Sunrise/Sunset offset)
   - **End Time** — when to turn off (optional)
   - **Repeat Days** — which days of the week
3. Tap **Save**

The schedule syncs directly to your WLED controller as a native timer, so it runs even if the app is closed.

### Auto-Pilot

Lumina's Auto-Pilot automatically generates a weekly lighting schedule based on:
- Your favorite sports teams' game schedules
- Upcoming holidays
- Your lighting style preferences
- Time of year and local sunset times

**How Auto-Pilot works:**

- A new schedule preview arrives **weekly**
- Based on your autonomy setting:
  - **Ask Me First:** You approve each week manually
  - **Smart Suggestions:** Auto-applies after 24 hours if you don't respond
  - **Full Auto-Pilot:** Applies automatically, no approval needed

You can always override Auto-Pilot by editing the schedule manually.

## 9. White Presets — My Whites

Your system uses **RGBW LEDs** — that means each LED has Red, Green, Blue, AND a dedicated White channel. This produces beautiful, true whites that regular RGB LEDs cannot match.

### Changing Your White Presets

1. Go to **System** tab → **My Whites**
2. You'll see the 5 built-in presets plus your custom settings
3. Tap a preset to preview it live
4. Select your new **Primary** and **Complement** whites
5. Tap **Save**

### Custom White

Use the R, G, B, and W sliders to create your perfect white tone. The W slider controls the dedicated white LED independently.

## 10. Remote Access — Control from Anywhere

By default, Lumina controls your lights over your home WiFi. To control them when you're away from home, you need remote access.

### Bridge Mode (Recommended)

If your installer set up a Lumina Bridge device:

1. Go to **System** → **Remote Access**
2. Toggle **Enable Remote Access** on
3. Tap **Detect Home Network** while connected to your home WiFi
4. Tap **Test Bridge** to verify the bridge is responding

That's it! When you leave home, commands automatically route through the bridge. The bridge is a small device on your home network that relays commands from the cloud to your WLED controller. It has its own web dashboard you can access at `http://<bridge-ip>/` to check its status.

### Webhook Mode (Advanced)

For users with a static IP or Dynamic DNS:

1. Set up a Dynamic DNS domain (e.g., DuckDNS)
2. Configure port forwarding on your router (external port → controller IP port 80)
3. In the app: **System** → **Remote Access** → select **Webhook**
4. Enter your webhook URL (e.g., `http://myhome.duckdns.org:8080`)
5. Tap **Save**, then **Test**

<div class="warning">
<strong>Note:</strong> Webhook mode requires a public IP address. It does NOT work with T-Mobile Home Internet, Starlink, or other carriers that use CGNAT. Use Bridge mode instead.
</div>

|---------|-----------------|
| **Controllers** | Your WLED devices — tap to switch, view details, or rename |
| **My Lights** | Hardware info — LED count per area, segment layout |

- **Edit Profile:** Change name, email, upload house photo
- **Security:** Change password, manage linked accounts
- **My Whites:** Adjust white presets (see Section 9)

### Simple Mode

If you prefer a simplified interface:

1. Go to **System** → **Settings**
2. Toggle **Simple Mode** on
3. The app shows only:
   - Home tab with large buttons
   - Settings tab
   - 3–5 favorite presets
   - Simple brightness control

Toggle it off anytime to return to the full interface.

### Adding Family Members

1. Go to **System** → **Manage Users**
2. Tap **Invite User**
3. Enter their email address
4. They'll receive an invite to download Lumina and join your system
5. Up to 5 family members for residential systems

## 13. Connection Status

The app shows your connection status:

| Indicator | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| **Green dot** | Connected — lights responding normally |
| **"Reconnecting..."** | Temporary connection loss — auto-retrying |
| **"System Offline"** | Cannot reach controller — check WiFi and power |

**If you see "System Offline":**

1. Make sure your phone is on the **same WiFi** as your lights
2. Check that the WLED controller is **powered on**
3. Try closing and reopening the app
4. Check your router — the controller may have a new IP address

## 14. Troubleshooting

### Lights not responding

- Verify WiFi connection (same network as controller)
- Check controller power — look for status LED on the device
- Force-close the app and reopen
- In the app: **System** → **Controllers** → tap **Refresh**

### App says "No controllers found"

- Ensure your phone has WiFi and Location permissions enabled
- On iPhone: Check Settings → Lumina → Local Network is ON
- Try adding the controller manually by IP address

### Patterns look different than expected

- Check brightness level — dim settings mute colors
- Verify which lighting area/channel is selected
- Some effects are designed for specific LED counts

### Forgot your password

- On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password**
- Enter your email address
- Check your inbox for a reset link
- Create a new password

### Remote access not working

- Verify Remote Access is **enabled** in Settings
- For Bridge mode: check that the ESP32 bridge device is powered on
- For Webhook mode: verify port forwarding and Dynamic DNS
- Test connectivity from the Remote Access settings screen
