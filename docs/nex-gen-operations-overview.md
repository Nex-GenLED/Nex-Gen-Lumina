## 1. What Nex-Gen LED LLC is

**Nex-Gen LED LLC** is a national permanent outdoor LED lighting company. We manufacture, distribute, and support permanent residential and commercial pixel-LED systems that mount on the exterior of homes and buildings — primarily on rooflines and architectural accents.

The product isn't seasonal Christmas lights. It's a year-round, programmable, app-controlled lighting system that runs every night automatically (dusk to dawn) and lets the homeowner pick from millions of colors, hundreds of patterns, and unlimited custom schedules. It's installed permanently — usually under the roofline soffit — so it's invisible during the day and stunning at night.

**Lumina** is the proprietary mobile app that controls these systems. It's the customer's interface to their lights, and it's also the operations platform our entire dealer network runs on.

## 2. How the dealer network works

Nex-Gen LED LLC operates as a **dealer network**. Instead of running our own crews in every market, we partner with local dealers who sell, install, and support the product in their territories.

### Each dealer is responsible for:

- Selling to homeowners and businesses in their territory
- Performing the physical install (Day 1 wiring + Day 2 lighting)
- Stocking inventory
- Customer support after the install
- Managing their own salespeople and install crews

### Nex-Gen LED LLC corporate provides:

- The Lumina app (everything from sales to install to customer use)
- The hardware (LED strip, controllers, wire, accessories)
- The customer messaging infrastructure (automated SMS/email)
- Network-wide pricing defaults
- Cross-dealer analytics and intelligence
- Training and onboarding
- Tier-2 customer support
- Marketing materials

Every dealer in the network shares the same brand identity (Nex-Gen LED) to the customer, even though dealer ownership is local. The customer sees a unified brand experience while the local dealer runs the day-to-day operation.

### Identifying a dealer

Every dealer has a unique **2-digit dealer code** (e.g., `42`). This code is embedded in the PINs that salespeople and installers use to log into Lumina, and it tags every job in the shared database so corporate analytics can attribute work correctly.

## 3. The three app modes

The Lumina app runs in **three operator modes**, each gated by its own PIN, plus the customer experience.

### Mode 1 — Sales Mode

**Used by:** dealer salespeople in the field

**PIN format:** 4 digits — first 2 are the dealer code, last 2 are the salesperson code

**What it does:**

- Create new prospects with customer info, photos, address, and notes
- Walk through the 5-step Estimate Wizard to capture every install detail (controller location, channel runs, power injection points)
- Generate professional priced estimates
- Capture customer signatures
- Track all jobs the salesperson is working on

**Session:** 30-minute idle timeout with a 5-minute warning

**Detailed guide:** Sales Mode Guide

### Mode 2 — Installer Mode

**Used by:** Day 1 electricians and Day 2 install teams

**PIN format:** 4 digits — first 2 are the dealer code, last 2 are the installer code

**What it does:**

- View the Day 1 Queue (signed jobs needing wiring prep) and the Day 2 Queue (wired jobs needing installation)
- Open per-job blueprints showing the home photo with channel overlays, materials lists, and task checklists
- Schedule visits and check off completed work
- Walk through the 4-step Day 2 wrap-up (photos, material check-in, customer account creation, close)
- Access the Dealer Dashboard for inventory and messaging configuration

**Session:** 30-minute idle timeout with a 5-minute warning

**Detailed guides:** Day 1 Electrician Guide and Day 2 Install Guide

### Mode 3 — Corporate Dashboard

**Used by:** Nex-Gen LED LLC corporate staff

**PIN format:** 4 digits, separate from dealer/installer PINs

**What it does:**

- **Network** — health overview of every dealer in the network
- **Pipeline** — cross-dealer view of every job, with stall indicators
- **Warehouse** — network inventory, demand, waste intelligence, reorder triggers
- **Admin** — dealer management, pricing defaults, network announcements, system PINs

**Session:** 60-minute idle timeout with a 5-minute warning

**Detailed guide:** Corporate Dashboard Guide

### Mode 4 — Customer (Lumina app)

**Used by:** homeowners and business owners after their install is complete

The customer downloads the Lumina app from the App Store or Google Play, sets their password using the link in their account setup email, and uses the app to control their lights. This is a separate experience from the three operator modes and is the "consumer face" of the platform.

## 4. The Lumina platform — what it is and what it does

**Lumina** is the in-house mobile app and cloud platform that runs the entire Nex-Gen LED LLC business. It serves four very different audiences from one codebase.

### For the customer

Lumina is the remote control for their lights. They can:

- Turn the system on and off
- Pick colors and patterns from a library of hundreds
- Set schedules (e.g., "warm white every night, red and green in December")
- Use AI-powered chat to describe a look ("make it look like a sunset over the ocean")
- Run dusk-to-dawn automation
- Receive software updates with new patterns and features

### For the salesperson

Lumina is their estimating and quoting tool. They can build a complete priced estimate on-site in 15 minutes and capture a signed contract before they leave the home.

### For the installer

Lumina is their work order, blueprint, materials list, and check-out tool — all in one. They never need a printed sheet or a separate ticketing system.

### For the dealer

Lumina is their CRM, inventory dashboard, customer messaging system, and analytics platform — all included with the product.

### For corporate

Lumina is the cross-dealer command center — pipeline visibility, network analytics, dealer management, and customer messaging configuration.

All four audiences use the same app. Data flows seamlessly from sale through install to customer use, with no manual handoffs or duplicate data entry.

## 5. The install process from 30,000 feet

A typical Nex-Gen LED job follows this path from prospect to live customer.

### Step 1 — Sales visit (Day 0)

A salesperson visits the prospect's home, walks the perimeter, takes photos, measures the rooflines, and uses Sales Mode to build a complete estimate live with the customer. The customer signs on the device before the salesperson leaves.

### Step 2 — Booking confirmed (Day 0, automatic)

The moment the customer signs, they automatically receive a booking confirmation email explaining the 2-day install process and what to expect.

### Step 3 — Day 1 scheduled

The dealer's Day 1 electrician sees the new signed job in their queue and picks a date. The customer immediately receives an SMS confirming the date.

### Step 4 — Day 1 reminder (night before, automatic)

The night before the visit, the customer automatically receives a reminder SMS with the access requirements (electrical panel, garage, exterior eaves).

### Step 5 — Day 1 visit (wiring prep)

The electrician arrives and runs all the wiring — controller wire, channel feed wires, injection wires. **No lights go up on Day 1.** Every wire is left accessible from outside the wall so Day 2 can connect to it without cutting.

### Step 6 — Day 1 complete (automatic)

The electrician marks the job complete in the app. The customer immediately receives a "wiring done" SMS.

### Step 7 — Day 2 scheduled

The dealer's Day 2 install team picks up the job from their queue and schedules a date. The customer receives a confirmation SMS.

### Step 8 — Day 2 reminder (night before, automatic)

The night before the install, the customer automatically receives a reminder SMS.

### Step 9 — Day 2 visit (installation)

The install team arrives, mounts the LED strip along every channel, connects to the wires Day 1 left, plugs in the controller, and powers everything on. They verify each channel works correctly and capture completion photos.

### Step 10 — Day 2 wrap-up (the most important moment)

Before leaving, the install team walks through the 4-step wrap-up sequence:

1. Capture install photos for every channel
2. Check in returned materials so the dealer's waste tracking stays accurate
3. Create the customer's Lumina account (sends them a password setup email)
4. Close the job (sends the customer a "system is live" email)

### Step 11 — Customer takes over

The customer gets two emails — the account setup email and the system-is-live email. They download Lumina, set their password, and control their lights for the first time. From this point on, Nex-Gen LED is their year-round lighting system.

**Total elapsed time:** typically 1–4 weeks from initial sale to install complete, depending on dealer scheduling.

## 7. How automated customer communication works

Nex-Gen LED LLC's customer experience is built on automated messaging. Every meaningful event in the install process triggers a message to the customer with no human intervention required from the dealer.

### The 8 automated touchpoints

| # | Event | Channel | When |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Estimate signed | Email | Immediately |
| 2 | Day 1 scheduled | SMS | Immediately |
| 3 | Day 1 reminder | SMS | 6pm Central, night before |
| 4 | Day 1 complete | SMS | Immediately |
| 5 | Day 2 scheduled | SMS | Immediately |
| 6 | Day 2 reminder | SMS | 6pm Central, night before |
| 7 | Customer account created | Email | During Day 2 wrap-up |
| 8 | Install complete | Email | Immediately |

### What the dealer controls

Each dealer has a **Messaging Configuration** screen where you can:

- Set your **sender identity** (the name customers see)
- **Toggle individual message types** on or off
- Set a **custom SMS sign-off** (up to 30 characters)
- **Preview** every message live before saving

Some messages (the operational confirmations) always send and can't be disabled. Others (booking confirmation email, reminder SMS, install complete email) are toggleable per dealer.

For full details on every message and how to configure them, see the Customer Messaging Configuration Guide.

## If something isn't working

**"I can't log in to my mode."**
Confirm your PIN with your dealer admin. If you're a new salesperson or installer, your PIN is assigned when Nex-Gen LED LLC admin creates your account.

**"I don't know which role-specific guide I need."**
Match it to the mode you log into. Sales Mode → **Sales Mode Guide**. Day 1 wiring → **Day 1 Electrician Guide**. Day 2 install → **Day 2 Install Guide**. Dealer admin → **Dealer Dashboard Guide**. Corporate staff → **Corporate Dashboard Guide**.

**"I'm not sure if an issue is mine or corporate's."**
Dealer-level: customer support, inventory, local marketing, hiring, electrical permits. Corporate-level: the app itself, hardware supply chain, network pricing, automated messaging infrastructure. When in doubt, start with your dealer admin — they'll route it upstream if it's corporate.

**"My dealer hasn't been activated yet."**
Contact your Nex-Gen LED LLC corporate account manager. Dealer activation is an admin action on the corporate side.
