# Nex-Gen Lumina: Installer Mode

## For Certified Nex-Gen LED Installers

---

## Overview

Installer Mode enables certified technicians to onboard new customers and configure Nex-Gen LED lighting systems during installation appointments.

---

## What You Can Do

### 1. Customer Onboarding
Create new customer accounts with secure temporary credentials during installation. The customer receives login details to access their system immediately after setup.

### 2. Controller Setup
Pair and configure WLED controllers via Bluetooth. Connect controllers to the customer's home Wi-Fi network for permanent operation.

### 3. Warranty Registration
All installation records are automatically logged for 5-year warranty tracking and dealer performance statistics.

---

## PIN Format

Your 4-digit Installer PIN consists of two parts:

| Digits | Description | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **First 2** | Dealer Code | `12` |
| **Last 2** | Installer Code | `05` |

**Example PIN:** `1205` = Dealer 12, Installer 05

Your PIN was assigned by your dealer administrator. Contact your dealer if you need a PIN or have forgotten yours.

---

## Getting Started

1. Open the Lumina app
2. On the Link Account screen, tap **"Installer"** under Professional Access
3. Review the Installer Mode information
4. Tap **"Enter Installer PIN"**
5. Enter your 4-digit PIN
6. Begin the customer setup wizard

---

## During Installation

### Step 1: Enter Customer Information
- Customer name, email, phone
- Installation address
- Site type (Residential or Commercial)

### Step 2: Configure Controllers
- Scan for nearby controllers via Bluetooth
- Connect each controller to customer's Wi-Fi
- Assign controllers to property areas

### Step 3: Handoff Credentials
- System generates a temporary password
- Provide the customer with:
  - Their email address (username)
  - Temporary password
  - Instructions to change password on first login

### Step 4: Complete Installation
- Installation record saved automatically
- Warranty period begins
- Customer can immediately access their system

---

## Important Notes

- **Session Timeout:** Installer sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity
- **Security:** Never share your PIN with customers or unauthorized personnel
- **Support:** Contact your dealer administrator for PIN issues
- **Master Code:** Nex-Gen administrators have a separate master access code
